# Jaw Harp Synth

A digital jaw harp (kubyz/vargan) synthesizer for iOS. Bringing the ancient sounds of the traditional Bashkir and Turkic instrument to your iPhone with modern digital effects.

## Features

### Authentic Sound Engine
Physics-based sound synthesis that captures the unique overtone-rich timbre of real jaw harps. Multiple tuning presets from different regional traditions.

### Touch Controls
Intuitive touch interface designed for expressive playing. Control pitch bending, overtone selection, and resonance with natural finger gestures.

### Effects & Processing
Built-in reverb, delay, and modulation effects to shape your sound. From traditional acoustic tones to experimental electronic textures.

### Recording & Export
Record your performances and export as WAV or share directly to social media.

## About the Jaw Harp

The jaw harp (known as kubyz in Bashkir, vargan in Russian, komus in Turkic languages) is one of the oldest musical instruments in the world. Used across cultures from Siberia to Southeast Asia, it produces distinctive overtone-rich sounds through vibrations of a metal or bamboo reed.
