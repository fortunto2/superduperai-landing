# Super Chatbot

Intelligent multi-agent AI assistant platform built for businesses that need more than a simple FAQ bot.

## What Makes Super Chatbot Different

- **Multi-Agent Architecture**: Specialized agents for different tasks — customer support, sales, onboarding, and technical help — all working together seamlessly.

- **RAG-Powered Knowledge**: Connect your docs, PDFs, websites, and databases. The chatbot retrieves relevant context in real-time for accurate, grounded answers.

- **Tool Use & Actions**: Beyond conversation — your chatbot can check order status, schedule appointments, process returns, and trigger workflows.

- **Deploy Anywhere**: Web widget, Telegram bot, WhatsApp Business, or custom API integration. One bot, every channel.

## Use Cases

### Customer Support
Resolve 80%+ of support tickets automatically with context-aware responses pulled from your knowledge base.

### Sales & Lead Qualification
Engage website visitors, qualify leads with natural conversation, and route hot prospects to your sales team.

### Internal Knowledge Base
Give your team instant access to company policies, procedures, and documentation through natural language queries.

### Onboarding Assistant
Guide new customers or employees through setup, training, and common workflows step by step.

## Technical Specs

- **Models**: GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, or your own fine-tuned model
- **Context Window**: Up to 128K tokens for deep document understanding
- **Languages**: 40+ languages with automatic detection
- **Integrations**: Zapier, Make, Slack, CRM systems, custom webhooks
- **Analytics**: Conversation insights, satisfaction scoring, topic clustering
