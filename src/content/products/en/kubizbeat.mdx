# KubizBeat

AI-powered music creation app for iOS. Make beats, melodies, and full tracks with a tap-based interface designed for creators on the go.

## Features

### Tap-Based Beat Creation
Create complex rhythms with an intuitive tap interface. No music theory required — just tap the vibe and let AI fill in the details.

### AI-Powered Melody Generation
Hum an idea or describe a mood, and KubizBeat generates matching melodies, harmonies, and basslines in seconds.

### Smart Arrangement
AI analyzes your beats and melodies to suggest full song arrangements — intro, verse, chorus, bridge, and outro — automatically structured for maximum impact.

### Sound Library
Access 500+ professionally designed sounds across genres: hip-hop, electronic, lo-fi, pop, R&B, and more. New packs added monthly.

### Export & Share
Export your tracks as WAV or MP3. Share directly to SoundCloud, Instagram, TikTok, or your DAW of choice.

## Who Is KubizBeat For?

- **Beatmakers** who want to produce on the go
- **Content creators** who need original background music
- **Musicians** looking for AI-assisted composition tools
- **Hobbyists** exploring music creation for the first time
