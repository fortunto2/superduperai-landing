## Key Features

Seedance 1.0 sets a new benchmark for AI-generated video fidelity with its advanced motion synthesis and temporal consistency.

### Dual Input Modes
- **Text-to-Video (T2V)**: Generate videos from descriptive prompts
- **Image-to-Video (I2V)**: Animate static images with motion effects
- **Hybrid Mode**: Combine reference images with text prompts for precise control

### High-Quality Output
- Generate 5 or 10-second video clips in professional quality
- Up to 1080p HD resolution with smooth motion
- Cinematic aesthetics with natural transitions
- No jittery artifacts thanks to advanced motion synthesis

### Multi-Shot Capabilities
Unique feature that strings together multiple scenes into one coherent video while maintaining consistent subject, style, and atmosphere across cuts.

## Prompting Tips for Best Results

### Be Descriptive and Specific
Seedance excels at understanding detailed prompts. Instead of "a person moving outdoors", try:
> "A young woman in a red dress dances gracefully through a sunlit forest clearing, leaves swirling around her"

### Leverage Multi-Shot Capabilities
Use connective words to describe scene transitions:
> "A robot walks through a dense forest, then emerges onto a busy city street with neon signs"

### Specify Visual Style
Include artistic direction for consistent aesthetics:
> "Cinematic scene with dramatic lighting in retro 8-bit game style"

### Include Camera Directions
Guide the perspective and movement:
> "Close-up shot that slowly pans around the subject, handheld camera style"
