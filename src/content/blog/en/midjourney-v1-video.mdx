## Key Features

Midjourney V1 takes a unique approach by focusing on image-to-video animation rather than text-to-video generation, leveraging the platform's strength in visual creation.

### Image-to-Video Animation
- Start with any Midjourney-generated image or uploaded artwork
- Animate static images into 5-second video clips
- Maintains original image quality and artistic style
- Four video variations generated per animation request

### Automatic vs Manual Modes
- **Automatic Mode**: AI creates motion prompts automatically for one-click animation
- **Manual Mode**: Write custom motion descriptions for precise control
- **Flexible Control**: Switch between modes to find optimal results

### Motion Intensity Settings
- **Low Motion**: Subtle, gentle movement preserving image clarity
- **High Motion**: Dynamic movement with camera and subject animation
- **Balanced Options**: Choose intensity based on content type

### Video Extensions
- Initial generation produces ~5-second clips
- Extend videos up to 4 times in ~4-second increments
- Create continuous videos up to 20-21 seconds total
- Seamless transitions between extended segments

## Motion Prompting Techniques

### Describe Movement, Not Appearance
Focus on how elements change over time rather than static descriptions:
> **Good**: "The person turns around and waves as camera pulls back slowly"
> **Avoid**: "A beautiful person in detailed clothing"

### Use Cinematic Language
Leverage filmmaking terminology for professional results:
- "Close-up shot", "time-lapse", "dolly zoom"
- "Camera pans left while subject looks up"
- "Slow motion with dramatic lighting"

### One Action at a Time
Keep motion prompts simple for V1's capabilities:
> **Simple**: "Dragon spreads wings and camera shakes as it roars"
> **Complex**: "Dragon flies through forest, lands on mountain, then breathes fire"

### Leverage Automatic Mode for Inspiration
- Try automatic mode first to see AI's interpretation
- Use results as baseline for manual refinements
- Combine automatic ideas with custom directions
